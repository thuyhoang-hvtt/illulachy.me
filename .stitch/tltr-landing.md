# TLTR — Landing Page Design

## Project Context

- **Project Name**: tltr; (Too Lazy To Read)
- **Type**: Desktop App Landing Page
- **Tech Stack**: HTML/Tailwind (landing page)
- **Purpose**: Turn web novels into audiobooks using your device's TTS engine—so you can listen during your commute, workout, or before bed

## Enhanced Design Prompt

**Atmosphere**: Modern, playful, and minimalist with a "sketchy zen" vibe. The design should feel hand-crafted yet professional, using clean negative space accented by bold hand-drawn illustrations and vibrant yellow highlights. It's about making reading (and listening) feel light, fun, and effortless.

### DESIGN SYSTEM:

- **Platform**: Web (Landing Page), Mobile-responsive (inspired by mobile onboarding flow)
- **Palette**:
  - Primary: Pure White (#FFFFFF) - clean, spacious background
  - Secondary: Deep Onyx (#1A1A1A) - for bold illustrations and primary text
  - Accent: Electric Marigold (#FFCE00) - vibrant yellow for CTAs, circles, and highlights
  - Neutral: Soft Pearl (#F9F9F9) - subtle backgrounds and card containers
  - Detail: Graphite Gray (#757575) - for secondary text and sketchy details
- **Styles**: "Doodle" style illustrations, stipple/noise textures for depth, perfectly round buttons (pill-shaped), high-contrast borders, and playful floating geometric shapes (circles).

### PAGE STRUCTURE:

1. **Header**:
   - Floating navbar with subtle paper texture background
   - Logo "tltr;" (book + play icon) on left, nav links on right: Features, Supported Sites, How It Works
   - CTA: "Download" button with warm Electric Marigold, no dropdown

2. **Hero Section**:
   - Headline: "Too Lazy to Read? Try listen!"
   - Subtext: "Turn any web novel into an audiobook instantly. Paste a URL, pick a voice, press play—then relax while your story comes alive."
   - Two CTAs: "Download Free" (primary gold), "How It Works" (secondary/outline)
   - Visual: A large, hand-drawn illustration of a book being "scanned" by a magnifying glass or opening like a portal, incorporating the Electric Marigold circle elements.

3. **Features Section**:
   - 3-column responsive card grid:
     - **Instant TTS**: Converts chapters in seconds, no waiting
     - **12 Voice Options**: Pick male, female, or character voices
     - **Export to MP3**: Save audio for offline listening anywhere
   - Each card: A sketchy illustration in Onyx with a floating Marigold circle behind it.

4. **Footer**:
   - GitHub link, Download buttons for Windows/Mac
   - Copyright and credits

### Visual Elements:

- Sketchy, hand-drawn "doodle" iconography
- Stipple/grainy texture overlays on illustrations
- Vibrant yellow circles as consistent design anchors
- Floating UI elements with subtle shadows
- Playful arrows and dashed path lines
- Micro-interactions on buttons (gentle glow effect)

### Typography:

- Headings: Outfit or Lexend (modern, friendly, geometric sans-serif)
- Body: Inter (readable, neutral sans-serif)
